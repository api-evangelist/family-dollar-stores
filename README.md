# Family Dollar Stores (family-dollar-stores)

Family Dollar is a chain of discount variety stores in the United States offering a broad assortment of merchandise including consumables, home products, apparel, and seasonal goods at low prices. Owned by Dollar Tree, Inc., Family Dollar operates thousands of locations across the country and serves millions of customers seeking value-oriented shopping. The company does not currently publish public APIs or a developer portal.
